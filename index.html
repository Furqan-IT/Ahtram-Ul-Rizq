ABOUT US

The goal of this project is to develop a food waste management website that will help individuals and businesses track and reduce their food waste in Pakistan. The website will provide users with tools to track their food waste, set goals and challenges, and receive recommendations on how to reduce food waste. It will also provide resources and information on food waste reduction, and connect users to local organizations working on food waste reduction in Pakistan.
